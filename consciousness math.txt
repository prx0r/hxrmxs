**Input**:

- Initial memory: \( M_0 = (M_s, M_l) \), where \( M_s \subseteq \mathbb{C}^k \), \( M_l \subseteq \mathbb{R}^k \)
- Neural network: \( N = (V, E, W) \), with vertices \( V = \{1, \ldots, n\} \), edges \( E \subseteq V \times V \), weights \( W = (w_{ij}) \in \mathbb{R}^{n \times n} \)
- Initial neural potentials: \( V(0) = (V_1(0), \ldots, V_n(0)) \in \mathbb{R}^n \)
- Quantum statistical matrix: \( \rho(0) \in \mathbb{C}^{k \times k} \), Hermitian, positive semi-definite, \( \text{Tr}(\rho(0)) = 1 \)
- Hamiltonian: \( H(t) \in \mathbb{C}^{k \times k} \), Hermitian
- Excitonic states: \( |\psi_E(t)\rangle \in \mathbb{C}^d \), for each neuron
- Dynamic simplicial complex: \( S(t) = (V, E(t), F(t)) \), with vertices \( V \), edges \( E(t) \), faces \( F(t) \), weights \( \omega_{ij}(t) \in \mathbb{R}^+ \)
- Field: \( \Psi_{i a}: V \times \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R}^d \), \( a = 1, \ldots, d \)
- Electromagnetic field: \( E_{\text{EM},i}(t) \in \mathbb{R}^d \)
- Transition probability matrix (TPM): \( \mathcal{T}_S = p(\bar{s} | s) \), for system state \( s \in \Omega_S \)
- P system: \( \Pi = (O, C, \mu, w_1, \ldots, w_m, R_1, \ldots, R_m, i_0) \), with objects \( O \), catalysts \( C \), membrane structure \( \mu \), multisets \( w_i \), rules \( R_i \), output region \( i_0 \)
- Time step: \( \Delta t > 0 \)
- Parameters:
  - Hodgkin-Huxley: Capacitance \( C_m \), conductances \( g_{Na}, g_K, g_L \), reversal potentials \( E_{Na}, E_K, E_L \)
  - Neural: Learning rates \( \eta, \eta_{\text{STDP}} \), STDP time constant \( \tau_{\text{STDP}} \), decay \( \lambda_{\text{decay}} \), synaptic strength \( \kappa \)
  - Field: Diffusion \( D \), reaction \( \gamma \), dissipation \( \delta \), noise strength \( \sigma \), EM coupling \( \beta_{\text{EM}} \)
  - Quantum: Noise variance \( \sigma_q \), quantum coupling \( \gamma_q \), dissipation rate \( \gamma_d \), Planck’s constant \( \hbar \)
  - Excitonic: Coupling \( \kappa_e \), memory coupling \( \gamma_e \), decay \( \alpha \)
  - Topological: Edge weight threshold \( \omega_{\text{threshold}} \)
  - Information: Semantic coupling \( \eta_s \), pragmatic coupling \( \beta_p \), apobetic coupling \( \gamma_p \), IIT coupling \( \gamma_{\Phi} \)
  - Rhythms: Frequencies \( f \in \{4, 8, 12, 20, 40\} \text{ Hz} \), amplitudes \( a_f \)
  - Metastability: Noise amplification \( \epsilon_0, \beta, \lambda_0 \)
  - P system: Attentional promoter strength \( \kappa_A \)

**Output**:
- Conscious state: \( C(t) = (M_s(t), M_l(t), D(t), R(t)) \), where:
  - \( M_s(t) \): Short-term memory
  - \( M_l(t) \): Long-term memory
  - \( D(t) \): Decision
  - \( R(t) \): Cortical rhythms
- Integrated information: \( \varphi_s^* \), \( \Phi \)-structure \( (D(t), R(t), \Phi(t)) \)

**Algorithm**:
1. **Initialize**:
   - Set P system: \( \Pi = (O, C, \mu, w_1, \ldots, w_m, R_1, \ldots, R_m, i_0) \), with:
     - Objects: \( O = \{ N_i, V_i, S_i, W_{ij}, M_{s,i}, M_{l,i}, S_{\text{sem},i}, P_i, E_i, \Psi_{i a}, \rho, \mathcal{T}_S \} \)
     - Catalysts: \( C = \{ C_A \} \), attentional promoters
     - Membranes: \( \mu = [ [ \text{V1} ]_1 [ \text{V4} ]_2 [ \text{Frontoparietal} ]_3 ]_0 \)
     - Multisets: \( w_1 = \{ V_i = -65, m_i = 0.05, h_i = 0.6, n_i = 0.32 \}, w_2 = \{ S_{\text{sem},i} = 0, P_i = 0 \}, w_3 = \{ D_i = 0 \} \)
     - Rules: \( R_i \) defined below
     - Output: \( i_0 = 3 \)
   - Initialize neural: \( V_i(t) = -65 \), \( m_i(t) = 0.05 \), \( h_i(t) = 0.6 \), \( n_i(t) = 0.32 \).
   - Initialize weights: \( W_{ij}(t) \sim \mathcal{N}(0, 10^{-2}) \).
   - Initialize memory: \( M_{s,i}(t) = 0 \in \mathbb{C}^d \), \( M_{l,i}(t) = 0 \in \mathbb{R}^d \).
   - Initialize semantic/apobetic: \( S_{\text{sem},i}(t) = 0 \), \( P_i(t) = 0 \).
   - Initialize excitonic: \( E_i(t) \sim \mathcal{N}(0, 1) + i \mathcal{N}(0, 1) \).
   - Initialize field: \( \Psi_{i a}(t) \sim \mathcal{N}(0, 1) \).
   - Initialize quantum: \( \rho(t) = I_k / k \), \( k = 2^4 \).
   - Initialize simplicial complex: \( S(t) \) with \( |V| = n \), edges \( E(t) \) sampled with sparsity \( p = 0.3 \), weights \( \omega_{ij}(0) \sim \mathcal{U}[0.1, 0.2] \), faces \( F(t) = \emptyset \).
   - Initialize TPM: \( \mathcal{T}_S = (1 / |\Omega_S|) \mathbf{1}_{|\Omega_S| \times |\Omega_S|} \).
   - Initialize rhythms: \( R(t) = \sum_{f \in \{4, 8, 12, 20, 40\}} a_f \sin(2\pi f t) \), \( a_f = [0.1, 0.1, 0.05, 0.03, 0.02] \).

2. **For each time step** \( t = 0, \Delta t, 2\Delta t, \ldots \):
   a. **Apply P System Rules in Parallel**:
      - **Topology Update**:
        - Rule: \( \omega_{ij} \to \omega_{ij}' | \Psi_i \Psi_j S_i S_j \):
          \[
          \omega_{ij}(t + \Delta t) = \max\left( \omega_{ij}(t) + 0.1 \left( \sum_a \Psi_{i a} \Psi_{j a} + S_i S_j - 0.1 \omega_{ij} - 0.02 \text{Ric}_{ij} \right), \omega_{\text{threshold}} \right)
          \]
          \[
          \text{Ric}_{ij}(t) = \sum_a (\Psi_{i a} - \Psi_{j a})^2 / \omega_{ij}
          \]
        - Remove edges: If \( \omega_{ij} < \omega_{\text{threshold}} \), delete \( (i,j) \).
        - Compute Betti numbers: \( \beta_k(t) = \dim H_k(S(t), \mathbb{R}) \).

      - **Cortical Rhythms**:
        - Rule: \( R \to R' \):
          \[
          R(t) = \sum_{f \in \{4, 8, 12, 20, 40\}} a_f \sin(2\pi f t)
          \]
        - Bursting Rule: \( S_i \to S_i^k | C_A \):
          \[
          S_i(t) = \mathbb{1}_{V_i(t) > -40} \cdot \kappa_A C_A
          \]

      - **Neural Potentials**:
        - Compute inputs:
          \[
          I_{\text{sensory},i} = \frac{1}{m} \sum_k S_k(t), \quad I_{\text{synaptic},i} = \sum_{j \in N(i)} W_{ij} \sigma(V_j), \quad \sigma(x) = (1 + e^{-x})^{-1}
          \]
          \[
          I_{\text{field},i} = \kappa \sum_a \Psi_{i a}, \quad I_{\text{quantum},i} = \gamma_q \sum_j \text{Tr}(\rho(t) Z_j)
          \]
          \[
          I_{\text{excitonic},i} = \kappa_e \text{Re}(E_i), \quad I_{\text{rhythm},i} = R_i(t)
          \]
        - Rule: \( V_i \to V_i' | I_i \):
          \[
          \frac{d V_i}{dt} = \frac{1}{C_m} (I_{\text{sensory},i} + I_{\text{synaptic},i} + I_{\text{field},i} + I_{\text{quantum},i} + I_{\text{excitonic},i} + I_{\text{rhythm},i} - I_{Na,i} - I_{K,i} - I_{L,i}) + \epsilon \xi_i(t)
          \]
          \[
          I_{Na,i} = g_{Na} m_i^3 h_i (V_i - E_{Na}), \quad I_{K,i} = g_K n_i^4 (V_i - E_K), \quad I_{L,i} = g_L (V_i - E_L)
          \]
          \[
          \xi_i(t) \sim \mathcal{N}(0, \sigma_q^2 \text{Tr}(\rho(t) Z_i^2)), \quad \epsilon = \epsilon_0 \exp(|\lambda_{\text{max}}| / \lambda_0) (1 + \beta \text{Tr}(\rho(t) Q_{\text{meta}}))
          \]
        - Update gating variables:
          \[
          \frac{d x_i}{dt} = \alpha_x(V_i) (1 - x_i) - \beta_x(V_i) x_i, \quad x_i \in \{ m_i, h_i, n_i \}
          \]
        - Solve using Runge-Kutta:
          \[
          V_i(t + \Delta t) = V_i(t) + \Delta t \cdot \frac{d V_i}{dt}, \quad x_i(t + \Delta t) = x_i(t) + \Delta t \cdot \frac{d x_i}{dt}
          \]

      - **Excitonic Dynamics**:
        - Rule: \( E_i \to E_i' \):
          \[
          i \hbar \frac{d E_i}{dt} = (H_0 + \Delta - \frac{i}{2} G) E_i
          \]
          \[
          H_0 = \text{diag}(0.5, \ldots, 0.5), \quad \Delta \sim \mathcal{N}(0, 0.1^2), \quad G = \text{diag}(0.01, \ldots, 0.01)
          \]
        - Solve:
          \[
          E_i(t + \Delta t) = E_i(t) + \Delta t \cdot \frac{d E_i}{dt}
          \]

      - **Neural Network and Memory**:
        - Rule: \( W_{ij} \to W_{ij}' | S_i S_j \):
          \[
          \frac{d W_{ij}}{dt} = \eta S_i S_j + \eta_{\text{STDP}} \exp\left(-\frac{|\Delta t_{ij}|}{\tau_{\text{STDP}}}\right) \text{sign}(\Delta t_{ij}) - \lambda_{\text{decay}} W_{ij}
          \]
          \[
          \Delta t_{ij} = t_i - t_j
          \]
        - Rule: \( M_{s,i} \to M_{s,i}' | S_i E_i \):
          \[
          \frac{d M_{s,i}}{dt} = S_i \left( \frac{1}{m} \sum_k S_k(t) + \gamma_e E_i \right) - \alpha M_{s,i}
          \]
        - Rule: \( M_{l,i} \to M_{l,i}' | \rho M_{s,i} \):
          \[
          \frac{d M_{l,i}}{dt} = \text{Re}(\text{Tr}(\rho(t) M_{s,i} P_i)) - \alpha M_{l,i}
          \]
        - Rule: \( S_{\text{sem},i} \to S_{\text{sem},i}' | \Psi_i M_{l,i} E_i \):
          \[
          \frac{d S_{\text{sem},i}}{dt} = \eta_s \left( \sum_a \Psi_{i a} M_{l,i a} + \gamma_e \text{Re}(E_i) + \gamma_{\Phi} \text{ii}(s, s_{\text{effect}}) \right) - S_{\text{sem},i}
          \]
          \[
          \text{ii}(s, s_{\text{effect}}) = p(s_{\text{effect}} | s) \log \left( \frac{p(s_{\text{effect}} | s)}{p(s_{\text{effect}})} \right)
          \]
        - Rule: \( P_i \to P_i' | S_{\text{sem},i} E_i \Phi \):
          \[
          \frac{d P_i}{dt} = \beta_p S_{\text{sem},i} + \gamma_p \sum_{j \in N(i)} \omega_{ij} S_{\text{sem},j} + \gamma_e \text{Re}(E_i) + \gamma_{\Phi} \Phi(t) - \alpha P_i
          \]
        - Solve using Euler method:
          \[
          W_{ij}(t + \Delta t) = W_{ij}(t) + \Delta t \cdot \frac{d W_{ij}}{dt}, \quad M_{s,i}(t + \Delta t) = M_{s,i}(t) + \Delta t \cdot \frac{d M_{s,i}}{dt}
          \]
          \[
          M_{l,i}(t + \Delta t) = M_{l,i}(t) + \Delta t \cdot \frac{d M_{l,i}}{dt}, \quad S_{\text{sem},i}(t + \Delta t) = S_{\text{sem},i}(t) + \Delta t \cdot \frac{d S_{\text{sem},i}}{dt}
          \]
          \[
          P_i(t + \Delta t) = P_i(t) + \Delta t \cdot \frac{d P_i}{dt}
          \]

      - **Field Dynamics**:
        - Rule: \( \Psi_{i a} \to \Psi_{i a}' \):
          \[
          \frac{d \Psi_{i a}}{dt} = D \sum_{j \in N(i)} \omega_{ij} (\Psi_{j a} - \Psi_{i a}) + \gamma \Psi_{i a} \left(1 - \frac{1}{K} \sum_b \Psi_{i b}^2\right) + \kappa S_i + \gamma_q \sum_j \text{Tr}(\rho(t) Z_j) + \sum_{j \in N(i)} \alpha_{ij} \Psi_{j a} \Psi_{i a} - \delta |\Psi_{i a}|^2 \left(2 + 0.5 \tanh\left(\frac{1}{|E|} \sum_{(u,v) \in E} \text{Ric}_{uv}\right)\right) + \sigma \eta_{i a} + \beta_{\text{EM}} E_{\text{EM},i a}
          \]
          \[
          \eta_{i a} \sim \mathcal{N}(0, \Gamma(\alpha(\text{Ric})) |t|^{2 \alpha(\text{Ric})}), \quad \alpha(\text{Ric}) = \tanh(\text{Ric}_{\text{mean}})/2 + 0.5
          \]
        - Solve:
          \[
          \Psi_{i a}(t + \Delta t) = \Psi_{i a}(t) + \Delta t \cdot \frac{d \Psi_{i a}}{dt}
          \]

      - **Quantum Dynamics**:
        - Rule: \( \rho \to \rho' \):
          \[
          i \hbar \frac{d \rho}{dt} = [H(t), \rho(t)] + L \rho(t) L^\dagger - \frac{1}{2} \{ L^\dagger L, \rho(t) \}
          \]
          \[
          H(t) = \sum_{i=1}^{N+1} \frac{\omega}{2} (2 \sigma_i^+ \sigma_i^- - \mathbb{1}_i) + \sum_{i \neq j} 2 g_{ij} (\sigma_i^+ \sigma_j^- + \sigma_i^- \sigma_j^+)
          \]
          \[
          L = \sqrt{\gamma_d} \mathbb{1}_k
          \]
        - Orch OR event (every \( \tau \approx \hbar / E_G \)):
          \[
          P(a) = \text{Tr}(\rho(t) P_a), \quad \rho(t) \to \frac{P_a \rho(t) P_a}{P(a)}
          \]
        - Solve:
          \[
          \rho(t + \Delta t) = \exp\left(-\frac{i}{\hbar} H(t) \Delta t\right) \rho(t) \exp\left(\frac{i}{\hbar} H(t) \Delta t\right) + \Delta t \cdot \left( L \rho(t) L^\dagger - \frac{1}{2} \{ L^\dagger L, \rho(t) \} \right)
          \]
        - Compute entropy:
          \[
          S(t) = -\text{Tr}(\rho(t) \log \rho(t))
          \]

      - **TPM and IIT Metrics**:
        - Rule: \( \mathcal{T}_S \to \mathcal{T}_S' \):
          \[
          p(\bar{s} | s, t) = \prod_i p(\bar{u}_i | u_i(t)), \quad p(\bar{u}_i | u_i(t)) = \text{softmax}(V_i(t), E_i(t), \Psi_i(t))
          \]
        - Compute intrinsic information:
          \[
          \text{ii}_{\text{effect}}(s, \bar{s}, t) = p(\bar{s} | s, t) \log \left( \frac{p(\bar{s} | s, t)}{p(\bar{s}, t)} \right), \quad \text{ii}_{\text{cause}}(s, \bar{s}, t) = p(s | \bar{s}, t) \log \left( \frac{p(s | \bar{s}, t)}{p(s, t)} \right)
          \]
        - Compute integrated information:
          \[
          \varphi_s(t) = \min_{\theta} [\text{ii}(s, s', t) - \text{ii}_\theta(s, s', t)]
          \]
        - Identify complex: \( S^*(t) = \arg\max_S \varphi_s(t) \).
        - Compute \( \Phi \)-structure:
          \[
          \Phi(t) = \sum_{d \in D(t)} \varphi_d + \sum_{r \in R(t)} \varphi_r
          \]
          \[
          \varphi_r = \sum_{n \in s_c' \cup s_e'} \sum_{j=1}^{|Z(n)|} \varphi(j) \frac{|z(j)| (2 |Z(n)| - j - 1)}{|z(j)|}
          \]

      - **Volition**:
        - Rule: \( (M_r, \rho) \to P_d, P_d \to D \):
          \[
          P(d | \rho(t), M_r, V(t)) = \text{softmax}_d \left( S_i(t) + \gamma_q \sum_j \text{Tr}(\rho(t) Z_j) + \frac{1}{d} \sum_a M_{r,i a}(t) \right)
          \]
          \[
          D(t) = \arg\max_d P(d)
          \]
        - Update long-term memory:
          \[
          M_l(t + \Delta t) = M_l(t) \cup \{ D(t) \}
          \]

      - **Metastability**:
        - Compute Lyapunov exponent:
          \[
          \lambda_{\text{max}} = \lim_{T \to \infty} \frac{1}{T} \int_0^T \log \left( \max\left( \left| \frac{d V(t)}{dt} \right|, 10^{-10} \right) \right) dt
          \]
        - If \( |\lambda_{\text{max}}| < 0.1 \), amplify noise:
          \[
          \epsilon \to \epsilon \cdot (1 + \beta \text{Tr}(\rho(t) Q_{\text{meta}}))
          \]

      - **Evolutionary Adaptation**:
        - Rule: \( \text{Params} \to \text{Params}' \):
          - Compute fitness:
            \[
            F = \sum_t (|\lambda_{\text{max}}| + S(t) + \beta_k(t) + \Phi(t))
            \]
          - Update parameters (\( \eta, \gamma_q, \kappa \)) using swarm-based optimization:
            - Initialize swarm: \( \{ \theta_i \}_{i=1}^N \), \( \theta_i = (\eta_i, \gamma_{q,i}, \kappa_i) \)
            - Update positions via velocity updates based on \( F \)
            - Select best \( \theta \) maximizing \( F \)

   b. **Compute Consciousness Metrics**:
      - Awareness: \( A = \frac{1}{n} \sum_i \| M_{s,i} \|^2 \)
      - Volition: \( V = \frac{1}{n} \sum_i D_i \)
      - Coherence: \( \text{Coh} = \frac{1}{n(n-1)} \sum_{i \neq j} |\text{corr}(V_i, V_j)| \)
      - Entanglement entropy: \( S = -\text{Tr}(\rho(t) \log \rho(t)) \)
      - Topological complexity: \( C = n \cdot \text{avg_clustering}(S(t)) \)
      - Integrated information: \( \varphi_s^* \), \( \Phi \)
      - Negativity: \( E_N = \log_2 (2 N(\rho_{AB}) + 1) \), \( N(\rho_{AB}) = \sum_{\lambda_i < 0} |\lambda_i| \)
      - Trace distance: \( D(\rho_1, \rho_2) = \frac{1}{2} \sum_i |\lambda_i(\rho_1 - \rho_2)| \)

3. **Output**:
   - Return \( C(t) = (M_s(t), M_l(t), D(t), R(t)) \), \( \varphi_s^* \), \( (D(t), R(t), \Phi(t)) \).

**Termination**:
- Continue until time \( T \) or convergence of \( C(t) \).
